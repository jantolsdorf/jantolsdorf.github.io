Data Cart

A human-centered privacy pattern for personal data management

Overview

Employees who handle personal data play a crucial role in protecting privacy and complying with strict data protection regulations. Yet, few tools have been designed to meet their needs.

Using a human-centred design approach, we conducted eight workshops with 19 employees from German public institutions and developed the Data Cart — a tool that supports employees in managing personal data while ensuring privacy compliance.

The Data Cart builds on the familiar shopping cart interaction pattern to simplify the complexity of data protection for employees, integrating privacy requirements into meaningful workflows.

Based on this metaphor and legal requirements, the Data Cart was developed as an employee-centric solution that synchronizes recurring tasks of retrieving and managing personal data with privacy obligations. The result is a harmonized combination of process flow and interaction concept, documented as a privacy pattern.
